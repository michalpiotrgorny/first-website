Can You imagine waking up every morning and see breathtaking landscapes through the window? Bitra is place like that! Views on Hekla mountain, bright sunrises and orange sky sunsets are worth to see.

Rich breakfast

Iceland is very demanding place - You need to be very well prepared and full of energy for your adventures. That's why we serv many diffrent types of breakfast cereals, bread, fruits, hams and cheeses!

Cozy rooms

After hard day every adventurer should take a rest. We provide clean and warm rooms with comfortable beds. We have diffrent types of rooms: family rooms, rooms for pair of two with private or public bathroom.

Location

Guesthouse Bitra is located in South Iceland, only 14 km from Selfoss town (10-minute drive) and 65 km from Reykjavík capital area (45-minute drive). It is perfectly located if your plan is to travel around Iceland as there are an abundance of natural wonders to explore, small towns to visit and activities available in the nearby area. Some of the well-known natural attractions in close proximity are Gullfoss, Geysir, Þingvellir National Park, Þórsmörk and the Westman Islands.
